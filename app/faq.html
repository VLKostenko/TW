What are Crypto Baskets?

Crypto Baskets are specialized Ethereum tokens that can hold diversified portfolios of other Ethereum tokens; think of it as a bundle of cryptocurrencies, easily bought and sold in a single transaction, without the need for an exchange.

Like ETFs and indices, baskets allow transacting portfolios of assets as a single unit

Instead of sourcing and buying each token separately, investors can buy a basket in a single transaction.

True ownership​: holders own and control their basket tokens and the portfolio tokens outright; no middlemen / custodian.

Each basket token represents tokens and quantities specified by the basket creator.

A basket token holder can exchange the basket token for the portfolio tokens at any time.

How do CryptoBaskets work?

CryptoBaskets are built on top of CoinAlpha’s Basket Protocol, an Ethereum-based suite of smart contracts that enables non-custodial, decentralized asset management.

Learn more about CoinAlpha’s Basket Protocol here:

https://github.com/CoinAlpha/basket-protocol

The Crypto Basket Ecosystem: Arranger, Supplier, and Buyer

The Basket protocol establishes three unique roles: Arranger, Supplier and Buyer. Each Basket's token composition is programmatically enforced by the Basket smart contract so that participants within each role are able to self-regulate their transactions. This process guarantees fair and safe exchange of cryptocurrencies between all parties.
